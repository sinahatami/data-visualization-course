Section 1: Linechart

Exploring Annual Temperature Fluctuations (1985-2023) in American States

Dive into the realm of climate data with our interactive line chart, offering insights into the annual temperature averages across American states from 1985 to 2023. Select a state, choose multiple years, and witness the monthly temperature variations displayed on the chart's x-axis, with temperature values measured in Celsius on the y-axis. Uncover the distinctive patterns and shifts in temperature throughout the years, empowering you to grasp the intricate dynamics of climate change on a month-to-month basis. Whether you're a climate researcher, student, or simply curious about the changing face of American weather, this visualization provides a user-friendly and informative platform to explore the fascinating world of annual temperature fluctuations.
