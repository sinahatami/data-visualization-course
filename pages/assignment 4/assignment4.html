Assignment 4 | Maps

Section 1: Dot Density Map

Distribution of Total Trees in the United States

This Dot Density Map visually represents the distribution of total trees across different states in the United States. Each dot on the map corresponds to a specific quantity of trees, providing an intuitive depiction of the spatial variation in tree population density. The color gradient enhances the representation, with darker shades indicating higher concentrations of trees. This map serves as a valuable tool to explore and understand the geographical patterns of tree distribution, contributing insights for environmental analysis and conservation efforts.

Section 2: Dot Density Multi Trees Map

Exploring Tree Diversity Across American States

This Dot Density Multitrees Map provides a comprehensive view of the variation in tree types across different states in the United States. Each dot on the map represents not only the total tree count but also differentiates between tree species using a diverse color palette. The unique colors assigned to each tree type visually illustrate the rich and varied forest ecosystems present in American states. This map serves as a powerful visualization tool to analyze the distribution and diversity of trees, contributing valuable insights for ecological studies and conservation planning. Explore the map to discover the vibrant tapestry of tree life across the nation.

Section 3: Choropleth Map

Total Areas and Tree Density in American States

This Choropleth Map offers an insightful perspective on the relationship between total areas and tree density across American states. Each state is color-coded to represent both its total area and the density of trees within that area. Darker colors signify higher tree density, providing a visual cue to areas with more concentrated tree populations. The map effectively communicates the geographical distribution of total areas and the varying levels of tree density, offering a valuable tool for understanding the landscape and potential ecological considerations. Explore the shades to uncover the nuances of tree density in different regions of the United States.
