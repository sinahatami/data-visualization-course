Section 1: Alluvial

Sankey Diagram of the tree families in the USA

The Alluvial diagram succinctly illustrates the relationships among seven key states, spotlighting one or two cities in each state. Within these cities, the diagram unveils intricate family trees, showcasing the botanical diversity through connections and scientific names. This visual representation offers a concise yet comprehensive insight into the complex ecological interplay within and across states.
